**Mobility:** Moderate. Daily commute to his bistro, biweekly market runs to Rungis. Occasional trips to Brittany or Lyon.
**Health behavior:** Active on his feet 10+ hours a day. Eats well, sleeps consistently. No health complaints.
**Social pattern:** Sociable at work, quieter off-duty. Close family ties, small circle of longtime friends.
